Empowering the community through quality technical-vocational education and skill-building programs.

Who We Are

TESDA-LLDA Provincial Training Center, a TESDA Technology Institution located at KM. 70, Brgy. Bangyas, Calauan, Laguna was inaugurated on January 30, 2020. TESDA-LLDA PTC was established to address the need for skills training program of the residents in District III and IV in Laguna, mostly those living in remote areas that are hard to reach by other training institutions.

Our Mission

TESDA sets direction, promulgates relevant standards, and implements programs geared towards quality assured and inclusive technical education and skills development and certification system.

Our Vision

The transformational leader in the technical education and skills development of the Filipino workforce

Our Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Social Responsibility, and Continuous Improvement in everything we do to serve our students and the community.
