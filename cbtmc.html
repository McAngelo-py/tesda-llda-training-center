What is CBTMC?

The Community-Based Training Management Component (CBTMC) is designed to address the specific needs of communities, especially in remote or underserved areas. It focuses on providing training that is flexible, relevant, and directly applicable to local economic opportunities.

This program ensures that even those who cannot travel to main training centers have access to quality technical-vocational education and skills development.

Why Choose CBTMC?

Our community-based approach offers unique advantages for local learners.

Accessible Training

Programs are brought directly to the community, reducing travel time and costs for participants.

Community Focused

Skill-Building

Focuses on practical, hands-on skills that can lead to immediate employment or entrepreneurship.

Program Overview

This qualification prepares trainers for community-based training programs, focusing on developing the training environment, organizing and delivering training, and reviewing training programs with a community-based context.

What You'll Learn

  • Training environment development
  • Community-based training organization
  • Instructional delivery techniques
  • Program review and evaluation
  • Facilitation skills
