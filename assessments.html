Assessment Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for your competency assessment.

Application

Submit your application form and required documents to our registrar office.

Payment

Pay the corresponding assessment fee as prescribed by TESDA regulations.

Schedule

Receive your assessment admission slip and schedule for the actual assessment.

Assessment

Undergo the actual competency assessment on your scheduled date.

Certification

Receive your results and claim your National Certificate if found competent.

Ready to apply for assessment?

For more information, please contact our assessment center at tesda-llda.assessment@tesda.gov.ph or visit us in person.
